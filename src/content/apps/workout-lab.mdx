# Transform Your Training

Workout Lab is designed for athletes and fitness enthusiasts who want complete control over their training data.

## Features

- **Smart Workout Tracking** - Log exercises, sets, reps, and weights with intuitive gestures
- **Progress Analytics** - Visualize your gains over time with beautiful charts
- **Goal Setting** - Set and track custom goals for any exercise
- **Offline First** - Your data is always available, no internet required
