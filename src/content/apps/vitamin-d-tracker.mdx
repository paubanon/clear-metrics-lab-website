# Optimize Your Vitamin D

Vitamin D Tracker uses your location, weather data, and personal profile to help you get the right amount of sun exposure.

## Features

- **Smart Exposure Tracking** - Know exactly when and how long to be in the sun
- **UV Index Monitoring** - Real-time UV data for your location
- **Personalized Recommendations** - Tailored advice based on your skin type
- **Health Insights** - Track your vitamin D intake and supplementation

## Privacy First

All your health data stays on your device. We never share your personal information.
